# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Dual audience: recruiters/employers scanning for candidates (need to quickly read competence and professionalism) and fellow developers assessing craft (notice details, code quality, originality). The portfolio must work for a fast skim and a careful read.

## Product Purpose

Thomas Stokes' personal portfolio at thomas-stk.co.uk. Showcases skills, projects, and background to attract career opportunities and professional connections. Success means a visitor leaves with a clear, accurate sense of Thomas's abilities and personality — and wants to reach out.

## Brand Personality

Dark, distinctive, professional, clean. Confident without being loud. The aesthetic should signal genuine craft, not a template. The cybersecurity and development interests should feel like one coherent identity, not two separate things bolted together.

## Anti-references

- Glassmorphism-heavy designs: blur cards, frosted glass surfaces, translucent layers as the default aesthetic. This is the saturated AI portfolio look.
- Purple/pink gradient text and gradient-everything palettes. Overused to the point of anonymity.
- Cookie-cutter dark SaaS aesthetics (navy/indigo + gradient text + particle background = no identity).
- Over-designed hype pages where motion and effects overshadow the actual work.
- Boring corporate light-theme resume-as-webpage with no personality.

## Design Principles

1. **The work speaks first.** Projects and skills are the content; the interface frames them without competing. Every decorative choice must earn its place.
2. **Distinctive through restraint.** Dark and clean means deliberate choices, not absence of choices. One strong identity decision beats ten decorative layers.
3. **Dual-register clarity.** A recruiter scanning for 10 seconds and a developer reading for 2 minutes should both finish with the right impression. Information hierarchy must support both.
4. **Craft coherence.** The design should reflect the same care Thomas applies to code — precise, intentional, no unnecessary noise.
5. **Motion earns its presence.** Animation only where it adds meaning or delight; never as default decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AAA. High contrast text, full keyboard navigation, meaningful alt text on all images, reduced-motion alternatives for every animation.
