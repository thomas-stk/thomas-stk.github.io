## Priority Issues

**[P1] Nested cards in Skills section**
- What: .glass-card-light skill cards sit inside .glass-card Skills section. Nested cards.
- Why it matters: Creates visual ambiguity; adds a surface layer that whitespace can do more cleanly.
- Fix: Remove .glass-card-light wrappers from skill items. Separate Frontend/Backend with spacing/divider.
- Suggested command: /impeccable layout

**[P2] No active navigation state on scroll**
- What: Nav links have no indication of which section is in view.
- Why it matters: Users lose orientation on single-page scroll portfolio.
- Fix: Use IntersectionObserver (already in codebase) to add .active class to nav links as their section enters viewport.
- Suggested command: /impeccable polish

**[P2] Body text line length uncapped in About section**
- What: About text spans full md:w-3/5 card width with no max-w constraint. Exceeds 75ch on large screens.
- Why it matters: Long lines degrade readability.
- Fix: Add max-w-prose to the About paragraph.
- Suggested command: /impeccable polish

**[P2] Skills intro paragraph is redundant**
- What: "Frontend in React and Tailwind; backend in Python, Flask, and SQLite." introduces cards that say the same thing again.
- Why it matters: Repeating information signals uncertain content strategy to a developer reviewer.
- Fix: Remove the intro paragraph. Section heading + two cards tell the story clearly.
- Suggested command: /impeccable clarify

**[P3] Missing OG image**
- What: og:title and og:description set, og:image absent. Twitter card is summary not summary_large_image.
- Why it matters: LinkedIn/Twitter previews show blank thumbnail.
- Fix: Add og:image with a 1200x630 screenshot. Update twitter:card to summary_large_image.
- Suggested command: /impeccable polish
